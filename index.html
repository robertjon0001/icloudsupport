Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my iCloud Mail password?

You can reset your password at iforgot.apple.com. You'll need access to your recovery email or phone number associated with your Apple ID.

Why am I not receiving emails in my iCloud Mail?

Check your internet connection, ensure Mail is enabled in your iCloud settings, and verify you haven't exceeded your storage limit.

How do I set up iCloud Mail on my Android device?

You can access iCloud Mail on Android through a web browser at icloud.com/mail or by configuring an IMAP client with our server settings.
